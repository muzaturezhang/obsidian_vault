#  Definition
根据 axioms 公理 build 整个system 的过程
1. define a system
2. from axioms(公理) (a few unquestioned, basic rules)
3. derive everything **logically** from the axioms 

---

# Creator of axiomatization of probability theory
 Andrey Kolmogorov

---
# Characteristics

With axiomatization, we build a system that:
1. precise (no ambiguity) (system内容本身)
2. consistent (no contradictions) (system 不同内容之间)
3. general (system的应用)
